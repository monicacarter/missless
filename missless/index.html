FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does Missless sound like a robot?

No. The voices are natural, context-aware, and trained on real conversations. Most callers cannot tell they are speaking with AI, and the ones who can usually do not mind because they are getting answers immediately.

Can Missless transfer calls to a real person?

Yes. You define exactly when and how. Missless can hand off a call mid-conversation with a full summary, so your team picks up exactly where the AI left off.

What happens after hours, on weekends, or on holidays?

Missless answers, qualifies, books, and follows up around the clock. You can set different behaviors for evenings, weekends, and holidays.

Will Missless work with my existing phone number?

Yes. You can keep your current number and route calls through Missless, or use a new dedicated line.

How long does setup take?

Most businesses go live in 5 to 7 business days. Larger multi-location or white-label setups may take a bit longer.

Is my customer data secure?

Yes. Calls, transcripts, and customer data are encrypted in transit and at rest. Missless follows industry-standard practices for storage, access control, and retention.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. If Missless is not working for you, you are not locked in.

Does Missless replace my team?

No. Missless handles the first response, qualification, and booking. Your team focuses on the conversations and work that actually close revenue.
